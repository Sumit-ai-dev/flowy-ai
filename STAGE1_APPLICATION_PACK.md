## Final Video Script (75 seconds)

### Section 1: Problem (0:00-0:15)
"Hi, we are the team behind Flowy. Product teams lose critical context every week between meeting transcripts, specs, and Jira boards. That gap causes avoidable bugs, rework, and slower shipping."

### Section 2: Build + Why Us (0:15-0:40)
"Flowy turns messy meeting input into structured product outputs. You can paste a transcript or record directly in the browser. Our orchestrator then runs specialized agents to generate an executive summary, draft a PRD, prepare a Slack update, and produce Jira-ready tickets. We built this end-to-end system in 72 hours, and it is already pushing tickets to live Jira projects."

### Section 3: Demo Proof + Traction (0:40-1:00)
"We handle quality with a reflection loop and version restore. PMs can refine the output in chat and instantly roll back to earlier drafts when needed. That keeps control with humans while preserving speed. We are currently at design-partner stage and actively use Flowy in our own sprint planning workflow."

### Section 4: Scale Vision (1:00-1:15)
"In the short term, Flowy saves PM teams hours every week on synthesis and follow-through. Long term, we are building a searchable memory layer for product decisions: what changed, why it changed, and who decided it."
